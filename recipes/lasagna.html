Four Cheese Lasagna

This Four Cheese Lasagna layers cottage cheese, cream cheese, mozzarella, and Parmesan for a rich and creamy bake. With tender noodles and a perfectly golden top, this comforting classic delivers cheesy, hearty flavor in every bite.

Ingredients

  • 1 lb extra-lean ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 pkg (8 oz.) cream cheese, softened
  • low fat cottage cheese
  • 1 pkg (8 oz.) low moisture mozzarella cheese, divided
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 jar (24 oz.) tomato pasta sauce
  • 1 can (14.5 oz.) diced tomatoes, drained
  • 2 tsp dried oregano leaves
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked

Steps

  1. Heat oven to 350â„‰.
  2. Brown meat with onions in large skillet. Meanwhile, mix cream cheese, cottage cheese, 1-1/2 cups mozzarella, 1/4 cup Parmesan, and egg until blended.
  3. Drain meat; return to skillet. Stir in pasta sauce, tomatoes, and oregano; simmer for 5 minutes. Remove from heat.
  4. Spoon 1 cup meat sauce onto bottom of 13x9-inch baking dish; top with layers of 3 lasagna noodles, 1 cup cheese mixture, and 1 cup meat sauce.
